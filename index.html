On strength

You are, without question, one of the strongest people I know.

And somehow that never crowds out how much you love being alive. Most people have to pick one. You carry both like it's nothing.

It's not the loud kind of strong. It's the kind that just keeps showing up, quietly, every single day — which is honestly the harder version.

On showing up

You showed up for me when it was hardest, no hesitation.

Not the easy kind of support — the kind that stays through the actual hard part, when there's nothing fun about it and no reason to stick around except that you wanted to.

I don't say thank you for that enough. This is me saying it properly, in writing, so it counts.

On being exactly who you are

You are a woman — fully, all the way. Not a girl still figuring out the part.

Grounded, sure of yourself, unbothered by things that shouldn't matter. It's one of the things I fell for and one of the things I trust most.

There's a difference between acting like you have it together and actually having it together. You're the second one.
